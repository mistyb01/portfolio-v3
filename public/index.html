A bit about me

I'm Misty, a 22 year old web dev and Seattle native! I use she/her pronouns. I graduated from University of Washington in 2022, with a degree in Informatics and concentration in human-centered design.

I’ve always loved the internet as a place to freely learn and express ourselves, and as a developer, I want to do my little part to make the web more open, accessible, and pleasant to use. I'm especially drawn to software that brings calm, and assists people in living meaningfully.

My career is just beginning, and I'm excited to continue learning from here! I'm seeking full-time work, and am open to working remotely, or on-site in the greater Seattle area.

When I’m not coding, I love to doodle, journal, read manga, and watch birds.
